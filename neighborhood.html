Ryan lives in Old East Dallas

Old East Dallas is diverse with several subdistricts. Ryan lives in the historic Junius Heights / Peaks Addition distric. It's a short walk to the famous Swiss Avenue block, which is full of impressive mansions. O.E.D is also directly in the middle of Lowest Greenville and Deep Ellum, making for easy access to some of Dallas' best restaurants.

Some of Ryan's favorite restaurants in Old East Dallas:

  • Jimmy's Food Store
  • Harvey B's
  • Aca Las Tortas
  • Thai Thai
